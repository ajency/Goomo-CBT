What happens once the request is sent for approval?

You will receive a mail that your request is sent for approval.

Your approver too will receive a mail that a request has been sent for approval

Approver will then either Approve or Disapprove the booking

If the Approver, approves the booking then you receive a mail informing the same. Similarly for disapproval

Once approved, you can go ahead and issue your ticket/voucher
